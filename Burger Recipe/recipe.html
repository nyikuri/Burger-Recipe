RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg Minced beef or 800g minced chicken
  • Salt 1tsp
  • Garlic 1tsp
  • Chilli Pepper ½tsp
  • Black Pepper ½tsp
  • Corriander ½tsp
  • Beef/Chicken/Garam Masala 1tsp
  • Bread crumbs (for chicken) - 200g/1:5 ratio
  • Bans
  • White onions
  • Mayonnaise
  • Brown sugar

    • Mix up all the spices to make a marinade. For chicken mix breadcrumbs first to make it firm. Progressively incorporate the marinade into the meat. Leave for hours in a refrigirator if possible.

    Divide into 10 equal balls to get averagely 100g patties. Crisp bans on both inner sides. Caramelise onions and sprinkle some brown sugar. Flatten the patty and cook on a pan or grill till well done on both sides. Apply a little mayo onthe botton ban. Lay a leaf of lettuce, then onions anf finally the patty. Enjoy.
